Delicious lasagna

Description

Recipe for a lasagna Garfield would be mouthwatering

Ingredients

  • lasagna sheets
  • creme fresh
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • 400g beef

Preparation

  1. Slice onions and fry with beef, add tomatoes.
  2. Pour into form and add lasagna sheets (alternate 3 times)
  3. Add creme fresh on top
  4. Put into oven for 45 mins
  5. voila!
